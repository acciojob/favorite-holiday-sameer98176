Manali - The Valley of Gods


Manali is a beautiful hill station in Himachal Pradesh, known for its snow-capped mountains, scenic beauty, and adventure activities.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Rohtang Pass - Famous for snow activities.
  2. Solang Valley - Known for paragliding.
  3. Hadimba Temple - A historic place.
  4. Old Manali - Popular for cafes.
  5. Manu Temple - A sacred site.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu - A traditional dish.
  • Trout Fish - A local delicacy.
  • Thukpa - A noodle soup.
  • Momos - Steamed dumplings.
  • Babru - A Himachali snack.
